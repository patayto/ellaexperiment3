You are going to hear a clip of someone talking. Based on how they talk, I'd like you to imagine what this person may be like.

For example:

  • What do they look like?
  • What clothes might they wear?
  • Where might they live?
  • What might their house look like?
  • What could they do as a job?

I'd also like you to think, "Do I know anyone who talks like this?"


Take 5 minutes to do this, you can write down any ideas if you want to, and feel free to listen to the audio clip as many times as you like.
